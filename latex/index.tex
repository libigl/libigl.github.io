\item {\bfseries{static library}} ({\ttfamily LIBIGL\+\_\+\+USE\+\_\+\+STATIC\+\_\+\+LIBRARY=ON} â†’ {\ttfamily IGL\+\_\+\+STATIC\+\_\+\+LIBRARY} is defined) --- speeds up repeated compilation.
\end{DoxyItemize}
