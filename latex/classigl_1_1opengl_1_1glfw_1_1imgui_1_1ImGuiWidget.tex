\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Abstract class for imgui "{}widgets"{}. 

A widget is something that uses imgui, but doesn\textquotesingle{}t own the entire imgui IO stack\+: the single \doxylink{classigl_1_1opengl_1_1glfw_1_1imgui_1_1ImGuiPlugin}{Im\+Gui\+Plugin} owns that and widgets are registered with it.
